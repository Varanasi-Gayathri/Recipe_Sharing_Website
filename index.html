Caprese Salad

Ingredients

  • 10 cherry tomatoes, sliced in half
  • 3 oz fresh mozzarella, cut to size to match cherry tomatoes
  • 6 basil leaves, Chiffonade
  • 2 Tbsp extra virgin olive oil, or to taste
  • 1 Tbsp balsamic vinegar, or to taste
  • 2 healthy pinches of Kosher salt, or to taste
  • 6-8 grinds of pepper, or to taste

Recipe

  1. Prepare the tomatoes, mozzarella and basil as specified, and place in a medium-sized bowl.
  2. Season with salt and pepper.
  3. Add extra virgin olive oil and balsamic. Toss to coat.
  4. Adjust seasoning as needed.
  5. Serve!

Chocolate Cake

Ingredients

  • 1 and 3/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 2 cups granulated sugar
  • 1 and 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 and 1/2 teaspoons baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 cup boiling water

Recipe

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour two 9-inch round cake pans.
  2. In a large bowl, sift together flour, cocoa powder, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
  3. Add eggs, milk, oil, and vanilla extract to the dry ingredients. Mix until well combined.
  4. Gradually add boiling water to the batter, mixing continuously. The batter will be thin, but that's normal.
  5. Pour the batter evenly into the prepared pans.
  6. Bake for 30 to 35 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
  7. Allow the cakes to cool in the pans for 10 minutes, then transfer them to a wire rack to cool completely.

Veg Sandwich

Ingredients

  • Cheese slices (your choice, like cheddar or Swiss)
  • Bread slices
  • Lettuce leaves
  • Tomato slices
  • Mayonnaise
  • Mustard (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Recipe

  1. Lay out two slices of bread.
  2. On one slice, layer turkey, cheese, lettuce, and tomato.
  3. Spread mayonnaise (and mustard if you like) on the other slice.
  4. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  5. Put the slices together, cut in half, and enjoy your sandwich!

Spaghetti Alla Marinara

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Canned tomatoes
  • Olive oil
  • Dried Oregano
  • Salt
  • Onion & Chilli flakes
  • Garlic

Recipe

  1. Gently fry garlic, onion, and chili flakes (optional) in olive.
  2. Stir in canned tomatoes, oregano and cook for 15 minutes.
  3. Taste and adjust the seasoning.
  4. Meanwhile, cook your spaghetti.
  5. Drain, add to the marinara sauce, toss well to combine.
  6. Serve with a pinch of oregano and a drizzle of olive oil.

Gulab Jamun

Ingredients

  • 1 cup milk powder
  • 1/4 cup flour
  • 1/4 cup ghee
  • 2-3 tbsp milk
  • 1 cup Sugar
  • 1/2 cup Water
  • 1/2 tsp cardamom powder

Recipe

  1. Mix dry ingredients, add ghee, and form a soft dough with milk.
  2. Shape into small balls and fry until golden brown.
  3. Prepare sugar syrup with listed ingredients.
  4. Soak fried balls in sugar syrup.
  5. Serve warm. Enjoy!

Vegetable Stir Fry

Ingredients

  • 2 cups mixed vegetables(bell peppers, brocolli, carrots)
  • 2 tbsp soy sause
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • 1 clove garlic
  • 1 tsp ginger
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Recipe

  1. Heat oil in a pan.
  2. Add garlic and ginger, sauté briefly.
  3. Toss in mixed vegetables, stir-fry until crisp-tender.
  4. Pour soy sauce, season with salt and pepper.
  5. Stir for a minute, and it's ready to serve!
